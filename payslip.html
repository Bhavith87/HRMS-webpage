Technical FAQs

Answers to common technical questions about our IT payroll solutions

What APIs do you provide for integration?

We offer comprehensive REST APIs with OAuth 2.0 authentication for payroll data integration. Our API documentation includes endpoints for employee data, payroll runs, time tracking, and reporting. We also provide webhooks for real-time notifications.

How do you handle data security and compliance?

Our platform is SOC 2 Type II certified with end-to-end encryption, regular security audits, and role-based access control. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other data privacy regulations. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit with AES-256 encryption.

Can you handle international payroll for remote teams?

Yes, we support payroll in over 50 countries with local tax compliance, currency conversion, and payment methods. Our platform automatically handles country-specific regulations, tax filings, and reporting requirements for international employees.

How customizable are the salary structures and payslips?

Our system offers fully configurable pay heads and components with reusable CTC templates. You can customize payslip templates with your branding and include detailed breakdowns of earnings and deductions. The system supports diverse compensation models for different roles.

What about technical support and SLAs?

We offer 24/7 technical support with a 99.9% uptime SLA. Enterprise plans include dedicated account managers and a 1-hour response time for critical issues. Our support team includes payroll experts with technical backgrounds to assist with any questions.
