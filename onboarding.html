Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our IT onboarding solutions.

How long does IT onboarding typically take?

With our automated system, basic IT onboarding can be completed in as little as 15 minutes for standard roles. More complex positions with specialized access requirements may take up to 24 hours. This is significantly faster than traditional manual processes which often take 3-5 business days.

What systems can you integrate with?

Our platform integrates with all major HR systems (Workday, BambooHR, ADP), identity providers (Okta, Azure AD), productivity suites (Microsoft 365, Google Workspace), and hundreds of other business applications through our API and pre-built connectors. We can also develop custom integrations for proprietary systems.

Is your solution scalable for large enterprises?

Absolutely. Our IT onboarding solution is designed with scalability in mind, capable of handling hundreds to thousands of new hires concurrently without compromising performance or security. We leverage cloud-native technologies to ensure elasticity and reliability for organizations of all sizes.

How does it improve security during onboarding?

Our system enhances security by automating role-based access control (RBAC), ensuring new hires only get access to what's strictly necessary for their role. It also centralizes account provisioning, enforces strong password policies, and facilitates immediate revocation of access upon termination, significantly reducing the risk of unauthorized access and data breaches.

Do you offer customized training for new hires?

Yes, we offer customizable digital training modules that can be tailored to your company's specific technology stack, security policies, and compliance requirements. This ensures new hires are not only provisioned with the right tools but also educated on how to use them securely and effectively from day one.

What kind of reporting and analytics are available?

Our platform provides comprehensive reporting and analytics dashboards, offering insights into key metrics such as average onboarding time, task completion rates, departmental efficiency, and compliance adherence. These reports help identify bottlenecks and optimize your onboarding process continuously.
