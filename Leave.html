Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Leave Management Software.

Can employees apply for leave through mobile devices?

Yes, our leave management software is mobile-responsive and supports leave applications via smartphones and tablets for convenience and flexibility.

How many levels of leave approval can be configured?

You can configure up to 3 levels of leave approval, including reporting managers, department heads, and HR personnel.

Can I customize leave types and policies?

Absolutely. You can define custom leave types such as casual, sick, earned, or maternity leave and set specific accrual rules and eligibility criteria.

Will employees be notified when their leave is approved or rejected?

Yes, employees receive instant email and in-app notifications upon leave approval or rejection, including any remarks from the approver.

Does the software provide a leave calendar?

Yes, it includes a shared leave calendar so teams can view upcoming absences and manage workloads accordingly.

Can HR generate reports for leave trends?

HR managers can generate detailed reports on leave balances, leave history, usage patterns, and department-wise leave trends to support decision-making.
